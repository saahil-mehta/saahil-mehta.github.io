# Experience

Seven years of persuading computers to do useful things with data. Started in Mumbai automating workflows that previously involved spreadsheets and prayer. Currently in London, where I migrate cloud infrastructure whilst everyone else is asleep and hope nothing breaks.

The pattern is usually: things are on fire → I fix things → things work → management forgets things were ever on fire → repeat. Sometimes I cut costs by 98% and they buy me a coffee. It's a nice coffee.
